MSI Academic Advising

This page aims to provide you with additional information regarding your specific degree requirements for MSI. The four tracks are Big Data Analytics, UX Research and Design, User-Centered Agile Development, and LAKES.

Fall 2024 Cohort Degree Requirements & Course Planning Materials

For Fall 2024, you need the following degree requirements:

  1. 39 SI Credits
  2. 48 Total Graduate Level Credits
  3. 3.0 to Graduate

To earn a Master of Science in Information at the School of Information, you must complete a minimum of 48 credit hours of graduate coursework, with a minimum grade average of B (3.0 minimum cumulative GPA required), including required coursework. Note that within the 48 credit hours of graduate coursework, a minimum of 39 credits must be coursework taken within UMSI (SI credit). Note: The AMDP has separate requirements. Please consult the specific AMDP Degree Requirements page for more information.

Instructions: Using the Course Planning Worksheets

  1. Make a copy of the Course Planning Sheet that corresponds to your chosen pathway (linked above) in your own Google Drive
    • To Make a Copy - Select "File" -- "Make a Copy"
  2. Select the correct plan at the bottom in the tabs available
  3. Add in any "Course Waivers" that you have earned in the "Waivers & Exemptions Relevant to MSI Requirements" section
  4. Plot out the rest of your coursework during your time at UMSI
    • Make sure to label courses appropriately so that the automatic calculator will do the work in letting you know if you will reach the credit minimums.
  5. Have questions or want your plan reviewed? Email your plan to the MSI Academic Advising team at umsi.advising.msi@umich.edu

Elective Options

MSI students must enroll in various elective courses to fulfill the 48 total graduate-level credit requirements for the MSI degree. Up to 9 of the graduate-level credits that count toward the MSI degree can be completed from a UM School or College outside of UMSI.

Internship Credits for MSI Students

MSI students are eligible to receive up to 9 credits for approved internships through the UMSI Internship program. Most internships are typically completed in the Summer semester between the first and second year in the program, with the credits backdated to the prior winter semester: Please contact the Internships team in the CDO for registration and support at umsi.internships@umich.edu.
